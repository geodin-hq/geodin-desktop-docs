# Workspace & Data Management

How GeoDin organizes your work: databases hold projects, projects hold objects (boreholes, locations), and objects carry general, layer, sample, well-design, measurement, and document data.

- [Connecting to a Database](databases/connecting-to-a-database.md) - create or open a database connection.
- [Working with Projects](projects/working-with-projects.md) - create, organize, copy, or transfer projects.
- [Creating Objects](objects/creating-objects.md) - add boreholes and locations and manage their data.
- [Working with Measurement Data](measurement-values/working-with-measurement-data.md) - enter, edit, and check measurement values and data types.
- [Managing Documents](documents/managing-documents.md) - attach files, photos, and reports to projects and objects.
